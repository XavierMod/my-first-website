Stop Eating Animals

One of the tasks for Digital Graphics was based on a book called "365 ways to change the world". As part of our assignment we needed to choose one of the ways and create a graphic using Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop. One of these ways was being a vegan for a day, so I picked the essence of that one and transformed it into a photo-montage styled poster based on the art of Raoul Hausmann, an Australian artist and writer.
