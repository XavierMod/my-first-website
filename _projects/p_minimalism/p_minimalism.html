Minimalism

As part of our Digital Photography assignment, we needed to choose a photography topic and take a series of pictures. I took a step further and I introduced myself into the "less is more" world. Minimalistic Photography was a challenge because I needed to apply strict photography techniques in order to get the best possible outputs. As a result, I took and edited six different pictures that thoroughly explored the core of Minimalism.
