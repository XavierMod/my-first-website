thebuilding:project

As part of the second stage of the Harlow assignment, I came up with a whole new brand that not only rebrands the city of Harlow but also encompasses other assignments. thebuilding:projects is a cross-platform architecture brand that extends to a logo, visual language, website and interactive map. Thus, this projects takes into account Digital Graphics, Creativity for the digital media and Web design all together.





The building:project is one of the first member-based city networks dedicated to promoting architecture, culture and lifestyle of its members through thebuilding rating system and providing them with recognitions and events such as thebuilding:awards.
